Building Carbon Emission Estimation System

Welcome to Building Carbon Emission System, a website developed by Ever Green!

In this system, you can check building carbon emission coefficients in the database.

In this system, you can estimate carbon emissions of buildings.

Estimator

The System supports two types of Carbon Emission Estimation of Buildings.

  • Life-Cycle Carbon Emission: You can input parameters of a building to estimate its life-cycle carbon emissions.
  • Real-Time Carbon Emission: You can check the real-time carbon emissions of a building.

Database

In this system, you can check four types of data in the database. The four types and corresponding record counts are:

  • Material: {{ num_materials }}
  • Transportation: {{ num_trans }}
  • Energy: {{ num_energy }}
  • Machine: {{ num_machine }}

Source

Data and building carbon emission estimation models come from the GB/T 51366-2019, Standard for Building Carbon Emission Calculation.
